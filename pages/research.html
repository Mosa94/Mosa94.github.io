Research

In my research work I apply various mathematical and data mining methods for several problems arising in distribution grids, renewables and smart buildings, such as:

  • Load Forecasting

  • Load Disaggregation

  • Load Modeling

  • Renewable Energy Sources Generation Forecasting

  • Optimal Dispatch of Integrated PV-Storage Systems

  • Profile Optimization with Storage Systems

  • Distribution State Estimation

  • Predictive Maintenance

  • Automated Fault Detection and Diagnosis

  • HVAC Model Predictive Control

  • Building Occupancy Estimation
