CV

I am PhD student in Electrical Engineering at the Intelligent Electrical Energy Systems (IEES) Laboratory, which is part of the Electrical, Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering and Naval Architecture Department (DITEN) of the University of Genoa, Italy.

I apply my statistical and mathematical education and experience to power systems topics such as renewable energy sources forecasting, intelligent distribution grids and smart buildings.

I hold a M. Sc. in Stochastics and Data Science from the University of Turin and a B. Sc. in Mathematical Statistics and Data Management from the University of Genoa.
